Intersting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Intersting Facts about Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Intersting Facts about Camels

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms
  • Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat

Intersting Facts about Bears

  • Bears are extraordinarily intelligent animals
  • Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing
  • Some species of Asiatic bear build nests in trees

Intersting Facts about Pandas

  • Pandas have five fingers and an opposable psueusdo-thumb
  • Pandas have vertical slits for pupils
  • Pandas spend 10-16 hours a day feeding

Intersting Facts about Cicadas

  • Cicadas live on all continents exept Antarctica
  • Periodical cicadas can live up to 17 years in the larva stage
  • A female cicada can lay 400 to 600 eggs before she dies
